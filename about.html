About

This application plots stock ticker data from January 2018 for 6 major pharmaceutical companies:

-Abbott Laboratories (ABT)
-Amgen Inc. (AMGN)
-Gilead Sciences Inc. (GILD)
-Thermo Fisher Scientific (TMO)
-AbbVie Inc. (ABBV)
-Allergan Inc. (AGN)

Plots display trends in closing price, adjusted closing price, opening price, and adjusted opening price.

Data is sourced from Quandl WIKI prices at: WIKI Prices

Tools used to buld this app include:

Heroku
Flask
Jinja2
Bootstrap
